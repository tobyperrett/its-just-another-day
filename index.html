1. Introduction

In recent years, image segmentation has seen significant advancements through the application of deep learning models. Traditional techniques, such as graph-based segmentation and clustering, have been surpassed by convolutional neural networks (CNNs) due to their ability to learn spatial hierarchies in images. In this work, we propose an enhanced deep learning model for image segmentation that achieves state-of-the-art performance.
